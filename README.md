# Haskell-Programming-Ebook

## 2.11 Chapter exercises

### Parenthesization

1. 2 + 2 * 3 - 1 =
    2 + (2 * 3) - 1 OR
    (+ 2) $ 2 * 3 - 1

2. (^) 10 $ 1 + 1 =
    (^) 10 (1 + 1)

3. 2 ^ 2 * 4 ^ 5 + 1 =
    (2 ^ 2) * (4 ^ 5) + 1

---

### Equivalent expressions

1. 1 + 1 == 2

2. 10 ^ 2 == 10 + 9 * 10

3. 400 - 37 != (-) 37 400

4. 100 `div` 3 != 100 / 3

5. 2 * 5 + 18 != 2 * (5 + 18)
